Newebe

Newebe is a distributed social network that aims to let you share while preserving your privacy. Because you host Newbe at your home, you keep control on your data.

technos: Coffeescript, Tornado, Couchdb.

related: teatime, a boilerplate to start quickly a web UI based on Coffeescript.

Twinkso

Twinkso is a social network for artists, specially, photographers, models and make-up artists. The principle was that you build a simple portfolio displaying 3 pictures, a short bio and links to your usual pages. Then you list people with who you work. This links to your partners offer a nice browsing experience to the visitor. This project in on stand by, maybe I will work on it later.

technos: Django, MongoDB

Dolebrai

Dolebrai was a webradio that brodcasted and promoted open music. There was monthly mix and a blog about open music news.
This project is finished, the website and the radio are no more available.

technos: Django, Liquidsoap, MySQL

related : Dolebrai Compilation vol. 01, Dolebrai Compilation vol. 02
